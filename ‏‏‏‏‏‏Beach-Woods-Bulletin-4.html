Beach Woods Bulletin, Edition Number 004, Sept. 4, 2019
Extra: Dorian Report

 

You may reach me, the presently self-appointed editor, at 321-768-0766 or garyhouchens@hotmail.com.

 

Dorian Report:

 

Chari (BW Office Manager) reported that the initial assessment showed little damage to BW other than downed limbs, etc. Our manager Jason was out assessing the property when she called, so some things may change.

 

The causeway across Indian River is open.

 

Chari and one or my neighbors indicated that apparently power was not lost in BW. (BW has multiple feeds for electricity, so one area may have been without power while others had it.)

 

Rain accumulation may not have been as great as expected, but that is an independent and unsubstantiated report.

 

Some phone land lines were out in BW.

 

Looks like BW dodged a big one.

 

Reporting from Lakeland, FL
