Summary of Rules and Regulations Effective 6/12/2008 Homeowners who disregard rules will be fined $100, and if not paid after 30-day grace period, another $100 will be imposed. For complete text see your Documents and the current Directory. In the event that there is a difference in the posting on the website and the directory, the directory prevails. See also the Board of Directors page for any recent motions made.
  • Trash. Pickup is Wednesday and Saturday. All household trash must be in Waste Management garbage cans. Cans and bins markings may be in black or white and be less than four inches in height. The BOD voted to allow residents to put trash out the night before the scheduled pick up day. But not before 4:00 pm the night before pick up.
  • Recycling and Yard Waste. Pick up is on Wednesday. Waste must be bundled, not bagged, and weigh less than 40 lbs. Waste materials cannot be burned.
  • Trash, recycling, or yard waste must be put out on the correct day. Waste Management garbage containers must be stored in your garage or court yard.
  • Quiet hours are between 10pm and 8am.
  • Parking is not allowed on the grass, blocking driveways or mailboxes, or on the streets. All vehicles parked outdoors overnight must have a decal. Vehicles parked on driveway must be less than 22 feet.
  • Boats, R/V's, and trailers are not permitted in driveways or parking areas without prior arrangements with the manager. No major auto repairs are allowed in these areas. No commercial vehicle parking is allowed.
  • Vehicles left in a parking space when owner is away for more than 7 days - manager must be notified.
  • Pets must be on a leash when on the common grounds. One pet per household. Owners are responsible to clean up after their pets.
  • Fences and balcony railings are not to be used as clotheslines.
  • Vegetation extending through or hanging over fence is the responsibility of the homeowner.
  • Clutter items (including garbage containers) must be stored inside fenced area, or closed garages, or they may be disposed of by management.
  • Garage doors must be kept closed, but on occasion the door may be left open 20 to 24" for ventilation.
  • For Sale or Rent Signs can't be more than 5 Sq.Ft. and must be posted directly in front of the property and not in the grassy area.
  • All exterior changes must be approved in writing by the Architectural Control Committee prior to commencement of construction.
  • No antennas, basketball hoops are permitted without prior approval of the Architectural Control Committee.
  • No skateboards, go-carts, golf carts ( golf carts only with BOD approval ) and motorized ATV's are permitted.
  • Bicycles, tricycles, etc. are not permitted in the pool areas, the river pier, crossover, tennis courts, etc.
  • Violation of the rules may carry a fine.
