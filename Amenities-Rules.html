Summary of Amenities Rulesr

For complete text see your Documents.

East and West Pools

  • There is no lifeguard on duty
  • Residents and guests only. Guests must be accompanied by a resident.
  • Children 12 and younger must be accompanied by an adult
  • Pool hours are 9am to 10pm. Covers are removed when temperature is 68F
  • Shower before entering pool
  • Infants must be in plastic pants
  • Glass containers, food, or alcoholic beverages are not permitted in pool or pool area
  • No diving, running, horseplay or ball playing in pool or pool area
  • No pets permitted in pool or pool area area
  • Rope must remain in place
  • Only noodles and wearable flotation devices are allowed in the pools
  • Capacity is 34 in the East Pool, 11 in the West Pool
  • We reserve the right to deny the use of the pools
  • In matters of dispute, the Manager's decision is final

Racquetball, Basketball and Tennis Courts

  • Residents and guests only. Guests must be accompanied by a resident
  • Hours are 8am to 9pm for Racquetball and Basketball, 10pm for Tennis
  • Players only permitted on court
  • Not for professional purposes
  • Players responsible for removal of water and debris
  • Soft-soled shoes and shirts must be worn
  • Beverages and tobacco not permitted
  • We reserve the right to deny the use of the courts
  • In matters of dispute, the Manager's decision is final

Clubhouse

  • No smoking or wet attire allowed
  • Open for scheduled activities
  • Library based on honor system. Keep books orderly, return borrowed ones. All book donations are appreciated. Obtain key from office.
  • Return furniture to its original position after use
  • Available for private parties (See Manager)
  • We reserve the right to deny the use of the clubhouse
  • In matters of dispute, the Manager's decision is final

Pier Facility

  • Residents and guests only. Guests must be accompanied by a resident
  • We reserve the right to deny the use of the pier facility
  • In matters of dispute, the Manager's decision is final
  • Hand launching of boats from boat ramp is at your own risk. Other launching is not permitted
  • Boat mooring at the pier is prohibited
  • Fish, shells, and other debris may not be left at the pier
  • The picnic table is not to be used for cleaning fish or cutting bait. The table and the pier should be cleaned after each use
  • Permanent mooring in the river is in violation of the state law. Violators shall be responsible for payment of fines and other related expenses levied against the Association

Health Club - Exercise Room, Sauna

  • Residents and guests only. Guests must be accompanied by a resident
  • We reserve the right to deny the use of the courts
  • In matters of dispute, the Manager's decision is final
  • Exercise room hours are 7am to 10pm. Sauna hours are 9am to 10pm
  • Children 14 and younger must be accompanied by an adult
  • Smoking not permitted
  • Footwear and shirts required in the exercise room. No wet clothing allowed
  • Equipment should be wiped down after use, and not moved around the exercise room
  • Exercisers should read instructions and learn how to use the weight machine before changing attachments
  • Sauna is not a changing room. No water may be used in the sauna.
  • If on medication, please consult a physician before using the sauna or exercise room
  • After using the sauna, please turn off the light and lock the door, shower before entering the swimming pool

Playground

  • Residents and guests only. Guests must be accompanied by a resident
  • We reserve the right to deny the use of the playground
  • In matters of dispute, the Manager's decision is final
  • Playground is open from 8am until dark
  • Use structures as designed, use at your own risk
  • No walking on monkey ring beam or horizontal ladder
  • No sitting on top rails or roof
  • Children 6 and younger must be supervised by an adult, all children supervised while on structure
  • Food and drink allowed in picnic area only
  • No glass of any kind is allowed
  • Slides are for going down, not up
  • Infant swings are for children 7 and younger
  • Big swings are for children 8 and older
  • No pets allowed
  • Keep the area trash-free, clean up after yoursel

RV and Boat Storage

  • Storage contracts and decals or ID's are required
  • Water and electricity are provided
  • Delinquency after the 11th day of the 2nd month will result in removal
  • Quiet hours must be observed
  • Manager will review the use of the facility to ensure spaces are appropiately used
  • Residents only.
  • We reserve the right to deny the use of the boat yard
  • In matters of dispute, the Manager's decision is final
