Summary of Rental Information and Restrictions

For complete text see your Documents.

Owners

  • All homeowners have the right to lease their unit
  • Upon rental, the owner surrenders his/her right to the use of all amenities
  • No rooms can be sublet or rented, and no transient tenants may be accommodated
  • No time sharing of units is allowed
  • Leasing does not release owners of their duties and responsibilities
  • All leases shall be in writing and contain names of owner and lessee, length of lease, number of occupants, out of town address.
  • Unfurnished units must be leased for at least one year
  • Furnished units must be leased for at least one month
  • Owners must provide lessees with a copy of Beach Woods rules and regulations
  • A common area security deposit of $200 is required
  • Owners are held accountable for damages caused by lessee

Renters

  • Must present lease to the Office to obtain a gate card, decal, and amenities key
  • Number of parking decals is limited to the number of spaces allocated to the unit
  • Must present to the office a valid driver's license and valid vehicle registration (s) to procure a gate pass ($30) and parking decal ($1).
  • ONLY ONE PET PER UNIT IS ALLOWED
  • NO COMMERCIAL VEHICLES ALLOWED ( CAN ONLY BE PARKED IN A GARAGE)
