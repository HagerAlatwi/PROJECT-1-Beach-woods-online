Summary of Amenities Rules
West Pool Heated pool with a river view, located next to the Hi-Rise building. Has bathrooms, shower, umbrella, lounging furniture and great sunsets! No life guard on duty. Open from 9am to 10pm when outside temperature is above 67°F. Capacity 11.
East Pool Heated pool next to the clubhouse, sauna and exercise room. Covered patio, bathrooms, shower, infant changing table, beverage vending machine, umbrellas, and lounging furniture. Capacity 34. Same hours and temperature guide as the West Pool.
Tennis Courts Three lighted fenced in concrete courts, spectator seating. Open from 8am to 10pm.
Ocean Dune Crossover Provides access to the beach. Benches, bike stands and recycled water.
River Pier Lighted; covered area with picnic table, benches, fish cleaning station, recycled water, boat landing platform.
Playground Sand box, swings, slides, climbing apparatus, jungle gym, picnic table, barbeque grill. Open 8am to dusk.
Boat Ramp Hand launch cement ramp on the river
Racquetball/Hand Ball Three courts, two enclosed. Open 8am to 9pm.
Basketball Court Open 8am to 9pm. Adult hoop and Children's lower hoop
Health Club - Exercise Room Open from 7am to 10pm. Weight and exercise equipment in East Pool complex.
Dry Sauna Open 9am to 10pm. In the East Pool complex.
RV/Boat Storage Fenced storage with water and electricity, nominal fee, in the boat yard.
Library In Clubhouse, honor system, well stocked with books, some games.
Clubhouse Function room with kitchen and bathroom. Water cooler. Big screen TV. VCR/DVD unit. Available for private parties for residents.
Bocce Ball Open 8 am to 7pm - In front of the tennis courts Must supply your own equipment
Plant Exchange Behind the Health Club
Garden Club - Native Plant Garden By the Bocce Court, in front of the tennis courts
Note: Amenities Key is required to access most facilities
