Landscape Notice to Residents

Beach Woods Landscape Committee is alive and well !! Patricia Tansey is the Chair, and the members are Frances Garrido, Marie Chertok, Tess Schmidt, Susan Melton, Ellie Horner and Judy Rohan.
The Committee is soliciting your input – let us hear your feedback on landscape issues or concerns. Please use the “Landscape Request Forms” in the office for work that you would like to have done. Your requests will be acted on as quickly as possible, as the Committee meets monthly -on the second Wednesday.
We have surveyed the property and divided it into zones, concentrating on landscape/maintenance, plants, mulch and sod, endeavoring to keep Beach Woods in pristine condition. Your suggestions are always welcome.
